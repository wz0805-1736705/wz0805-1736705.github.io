Most Popular EDM Festival

Ultra Music Festival is an annual outdoor electronic music festival that takes place during March in the city of Miami, Florida.[2] The festival was founded in 1999 by Russell Faibisch and Alex Omes and is named after the 1997 Depeche Mode album, Ultra.

Tomorrowland is an electronic dance music festival held in Boom, Belgium. Tomorrowland was first held in 2005, and has since become one of the world's largest and most notable music festivals. It now stretches over 2 weekends and usually sells out in minutes.

Electric Daisy Carnival, commonly known as EDC, is one of the biggest electronic dance music festivals in the world, based on attendance, with its flagship event held annually in Las Vegas, Nevada.
